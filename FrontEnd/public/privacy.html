Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Junk 2 Go ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or communicate with us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Service address
  • Photos and descriptions of items you need removed
  • Any other information you share during a quote conversation

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide junk removal quotes and schedule pickups
  • Send SMS messages related to your quote and service (see SMS section below)
  • Respond to your questions and requests
  • Improve our services

3. SMS / Text Message Communications

SMS Program: By providing your phone number to Junk 2 Go, you consent to receive transactional SMS text messages from us regarding your quote and service, including quote approvals, scheduling confirmations, and service updates.

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies. You may receive up to a few messages per service request.

Message & Data Rates: Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out: You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

Help: Reply HELP for assistance or contact us at (734) 308-7600.

No Purchase Required: Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchasing any service.

Carriers: Supported carriers include AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and most major US carriers. Carrier support may vary.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Our SMS service provider (Vonage) solely to deliver text messages you have consented to receive
  • Service providers who assist us in operating our business, subject to confidentiality obligations
  • Law enforcement or government agencies when required by law

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, provide ongoing services, or as required by law. Quote conversation data is generally retained for up to 12 months.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
